Regular Expression Testing

This app will remove anything found with your regular expression and replace it with '' - that is, nothing. Basically it just removes any matches, prints the result, then prints the result as an array of characters.

I made this because I'm struggling with regular expressions and wanted a way to test them out. It'll probably change as I get a better understanding of how to work with them.
